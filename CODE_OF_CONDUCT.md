# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and give actionable feedback.
- Focus on technical outcomes, not personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination
- Personal insults or abusive language
- Doxxing or sharing private information

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code.
They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and issues that do not align with this policy.
